# Template Disclaimer Guide: CiviCore Resource and Template Library

## 1. Purpose

This guide defines disclaimer patterns and placement rules for templates, guides, calculators, and downloadable resources published by the CiviCore Resource and Template Library.

Disclaimers help clarify that resources are provided for general reference, drafting assistance, and practical guidance. They should reduce the risk that users mistake templates for legal advice, official forms, mandatory language, or guaranteed compliance documents.

## 2. General Disclaimer

Use this disclaimer for general templates, guides, checklists, and sample documents:

```text
This resource is provided for general reference and drafting assistance only. Users should review and adapt it according to their office policies, applicable laws, and approval procedures.
```

## 3. Legal-Adjacent Template Disclaimer

Use this disclaimer for right-of-way agreements, permit-to-enter forms, waivers, consent documents, MOAs, MOUs, contracts, agreement-style templates, and similar resources:

```text
This template is provided for general reference and drafting assistance only. It is not legal advice and does not guarantee legal sufficiency for any specific situation. Users should review and adapt it according to their office policies, applicable laws, approval procedures, and, where appropriate, guidance from an authorized legal adviser.
```

## 4. Project Proposal Disclaimer

Use this disclaimer for project proposals, concept notes, costed proposals, funding request templates, and related planning documents:

```text
This project proposal template is provided for general planning and drafting assistance only. Users should review and adapt it based on their office requirements, project scope, funding rules, technical standards, and approval procedures.
```

## 5. Calculator/Tool Disclaimer

Use this disclaimer for calculators, estimators, readiness checklists, recommendation tools, and similar resources:

```text
This tool is provided for general estimation and planning assistance only. Results should be reviewed and validated before being used for budgeting, procurement, billing, compliance, or official decision-making.
```

## 6. Downloadable Template Pack Disclaimer

Use this disclaimer for downloadable packs, bundled templates, paid packs, and future premium resources:

```text
This downloadable template pack is provided for general reference and drafting assistance only. The templates are not legal advice, official government forms, or guaranteed compliance documents. Users are responsible for reviewing, adapting, and approving each template according to their office policies, applicable laws, and approval procedures.
```

## 7. Placement Rules

Disclaimers should be visible near the template introduction.

Disclaimers should be repeated in downloadable files when practical, especially for legal-adjacent documents, proposal templates, calculators, and premium template packs.

For legal-adjacent resources, add the disclaimer before the template text so users see the caution before copying or adapting the document.

For calculators and tools, place the disclaimer near the input/output area and any downloadable result.

For project proposals and cost estimates, place the disclaimer before the sample proposal or estimate table.

## 8. Content That Requires Disclaimer

The following content types require disclaimers:

- Right-of-way agreements
- Permit-to-enter forms
- Contracts
- MOAs/MOUs
- Waivers
- Consent documents
- Project proposals
- Cost estimate calculators
- Procurement/compliance-related guides
- Funding request templates
- Agreement-style templates
- Billing calculators
- Readiness assessment tools

Legal-adjacent and compliance-related resources should use the stronger disclaimer and require extra review before publication.
