## General Template Disclaimer

Users should review and adapt each resource according to their office policies, applicable requirements, local context, and approval procedures.

Some resources may involve legal-adjacent topics, such as right-of-way agreements, permit-to-enter forms, consent documents, waivers, MOAs, MOUs, contracts, or other agreement-style templates.

These resources are not legal advice and do not guarantee legal sufficiency for any specific situation.

Users should review legal-adjacent documents with the appropriate office, approving authority, or legal adviser where applicable.

Project proposal templates, budget summaries, and cost estimate formats are provided for planning and drafting assistance only.

They are not official budgeting, engineering, procurement, accounting, technical, or compliance documents.

The CiviCore Resource and Template Library does not provide legal, accounting, engineering, procurement, cybersecurity, data privacy, or compliance advice.

The resources do not guarantee approval, compliance, funding, legal sufficiency, or technical correctness for any specific case.

Users are responsible for reviewing, adapting, validating, and approving any resource before official use.
