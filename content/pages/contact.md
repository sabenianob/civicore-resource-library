# Contact

Suggested Slug: /contact/

## General Contact Purpose

Use this page to contact CiviCore IT Solutions regarding resource questions, template customization inquiries, website modernization, office digitalization planning, or related service inquiries.

This contact page should be simple, respectful, and practical. It should not pressure visitors into unnecessary services.

## Suggested Contact Details

Replace these placeholders before launch:

```text
Organization: CiviCore IT Solutions
Email: [official email address]
Phone / Mobile: [official contact number]
Office Location: [office location, if intended for publication]
Business Hours: [business hours]
Website: [main CiviCore website URL]
```

## Template Customization Inquiries

Users may contact CiviCore if they need help adapting a template for a specific office workflow, project type, or documentation need.

Suggested inquiry details:

- Template or guide name
- Office or organization type
- Purpose of the document
- Needed changes or customization
- Target timeline, if any

## CiviCore Service Inquiries

CiviCore may assist with practical digitalization and systems-related work such as:

- Website planning or modernization
- Office digitalization workflow planning
- Document template organization
- Shared folder or Google Workspace structure
- Public-service forms and content organization
- Simple system planning and implementation support

## Contact Form Note

If a contact form is added later, it should collect only necessary information and include a privacy notice.

Suggested fields:

- Name
- Office / Organization
- Email or contact number
- Inquiry type
- Message

Avoid collecting sensitive personal information through the contact form unless there is a clear reason, proper authority, and appropriate safeguards.

## No Aggressive Lead Capture

The contact page should support legitimate inquiries without aggressive sales language, forced signups, or unnecessary data collection.
