# About CiviCore Resources

Suggested Slug: /about/

## What the Resource Library Is

CiviCore Resource and Template Library is a public collection of practical templates, guides, checklists, sample documents, and planning tools for local public-service work.

The library is designed to help offices prepare common documents, organize project information, improve basic workflows, and start practical digitalization efforts.

## Who It Is For

This resource library is intended for:

- LGU personnel
- Barangay officials and staff
- Water district staff
- Administrative personnel
- Planning and development office staff
- Small public-service organizations
- Cooperatives, NGOs, and community organizations

## What Kinds of Resources Are Available

The library may include:

- Formal letter templates
- Barangay and LGU project proposal templates
- Right-of-way and permit-to-enter reference templates
- Water district resources
- Office digitalization guides
- Google Workspace and shared folder guides
- Cost estimate templates
- Checklists and simple planning tools

## CiviCore IT Solutions Positioning

CiviCore IT Solutions supports practical digitalization, documentation, and systems improvement for local institutions.

The resource library is part of CiviCore's effort to provide useful public materials for offices that need clearer templates, better workflows, and more organized digital processes.

## Reference and Drafting Assistance Note

Templates and guides in this library are provided for general reference, planning, and drafting assistance only.

Users should review and adapt each resource according to their office policies, applicable requirements, technical needs, approval procedures, and local context.

Legal-adjacent, technical, budgeting, procurement, privacy, and compliance-related resources should receive appropriate review before official use.
