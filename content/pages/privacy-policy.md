# Privacy Policy

Suggested Slug: /privacy-policy/

## Starter Privacy Policy Draft

This is a starter privacy policy draft for the CiviCore Resource and Template Library.

This draft must be reviewed before public launch, especially if analytics, cookies, contact forms, newsletter signup, downloads, lead capture, payment tools, or third-party services are enabled.

## Information We May Collect

Depending on the final website setup, the resource library may collect:

- Name
- Email address
- Contact number
- Office or organization name
- Inquiry message
- Resource request or template customization details
- Website usage information through analytics, if enabled
- Technical information such as browser, device, pages visited, and approximate location, if analytics are enabled

The website should collect only information that is necessary for the intended purpose.

## How Information May Be Used

Information may be used to:

- Respond to inquiries
- Provide requested information
- Review template customization or service inquiries
- Improve website content and usability
- Understand which resources are useful to visitors
- Maintain website security and reliability
- Comply with applicable requirements, where necessary

Personal information should not be sold or used for unrelated purposes.

## Cookies / Analytics Placeholder

If analytics, cookies, tracking tools, or similar technologies are added, this section should be updated before launch.

Possible analytics uses may include:

- Measuring page visits
- Understanding popular resources
- Reviewing search and traffic patterns
- Improving website structure and content

Analytics should be configured with privacy and data minimization in mind.

## Contact Form Placeholder

If a contact form is added, the website should explain:

- What information is collected
- Why the information is collected
- Who receives the inquiry
- How long information may be retained
- How users may request correction or deletion, where applicable

Contact forms should avoid collecting sensitive personal information unless necessary and properly safeguarded.

## Third-Party Services Placeholder

If third-party services are used, this policy should identify them where appropriate.

Possible third-party services may include:

- Website hosting provider
- Analytics provider
- Email or contact form provider
- Spam protection service
- Backup service
- Payment or download service, if added later

Third-party services should be reviewed for privacy, security, and data handling practices before use.

## User Responsibility Note

Users should avoid submitting sensitive personal information, confidential office records, private legal documents, or procurement-sensitive information through general contact forms unless specifically requested through an appropriate and secure process.

## Review-Before-Launch Note

This privacy policy is a practical starter draft only.

Before public launch, it should be reviewed and updated based on the actual website configuration, including analytics, cookies, forms, data collection, third-party services, lead capture, downloads, and any other processing of personal information.
